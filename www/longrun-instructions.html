This app shows how the cumulative proportion of successes changes in a binomial experiment. Each trial has one of two outcomes: “success” or “failure”.

  • Enter the success probability for your experiment in the grey box.
  • Clicking the Simulate button will run the specified number of trials of your experiment and append the outcomes to the results list on the far right.
  • A graph of the cumulative proportion of successes achieved after each trial is plotted in the middle with a horizontal line that corresponds with the success probability.
  • Unchecking the Animate box will display the simulation results for all trials immediately instead of updating the graph after each trial.
